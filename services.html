What we do

Software Development

We develop software applications using the most varied
technologies; Android mobile applications connected on
Java and Kotlin; IoS applications, QT applications, and
cloud applications. For our software products we maintain
an automated test suite and continuous integration on
Jenkins to ensure product quality.

More

Embeded Systems

We design and develop integrated systems by
providing services such as: bare metal firmware
development, microcontroller and microprocessor
board design, BSP writing. We specialize in devices
managed on Linux including Android and Yocto
operating systems, custom firmware development
for microcontrollers including Zephyr OS, freeRtos.

Hardware Development

We participate in any phase of the product
development cycle, from conception to prototype,
from hardware design, to the production of samples
and the production of small quantities of electronic
boards. Provide product pre-compliance tests and
hardware tests.
